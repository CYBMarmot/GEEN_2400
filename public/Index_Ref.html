The Fan Club
This project has several goals in regards to the challenge presented by the Jump Into Stem Competition.
Improvement to decision making with regards to open-access air quality data.
Advocation for the development and maintenace of more complex autonomous systems to address specific air quality needs.
Identification and relevance of natural and man-made hazards in real time.
Advocacy for target groups affected by air quality.
Development of research into sustainable air quality mechanisms.
Advocacy for the retrofitting of existing systems.
